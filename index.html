About This Guide

This guide is designed for clarity and quick reference. It is structured to help you understand the role of each component within a complete system.

The Three Primary Functions of an Electronic System:

  • Controller (The Brain): Executes code and manages the system. This is where your intelligence lives.
  • Sensors (The Senses): Gathers input from the environment (temperature, light, motion, etc.).
  • Actuators (The Muscles): Executes physical actions or displays output (turning a motor, lighting an LED, showing text on a screen).
